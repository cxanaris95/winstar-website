Types of Mirrors

Regular

Regular mirrors feature a smooth, flat reflective surface with clean, straight-cut edges. Available in a wide range of sizes and custom dimensions, they are the most versatile mirror option and can be used in virtually any setting — from bathrooms, bedrooms, and hallways to retail environments and commercial spaces. Regular mirrors provide clear, true-color reflection and can be frameless or paired with a frame of your choice.

Beveled

Beveled mirrors are distinguished by their angled, polished edges, which create a decorative border around the perimeter of the mirror. The bevel refracts light and adds a prismatic, elegant effect that enhances the appearance of any room. Available in a range of bevel widths, beveled mirrors are a popular choice for bathrooms, dressing rooms, entryways, and decorative wall installations where a refined, finished look is desired.

Antique

Antique mirrors feature a deliberately aged or distressed reflective surface that gives the glass a warm, vintage character. The subtle imperfections and tonal variation in the reflective coating evoke a sense of history and craftsmanship, making antique mirrors a striking decorative element in both classic and contemporary interiors. They are widely used as accent pieces, feature walls, cabinet inserts, and in high-end residential and hospitality design.
