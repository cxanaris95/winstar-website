Types of Shower Doors

DS21 Series

The DS21 Series frameless sliding shower door combines a clean, modern aesthetic with lasting durability. Crafted from 3/8" clear tempered glass and fitted with high-quality hardware, this two-piece system pairs a fixed panel with a sliding door for a sleek, minimalist look.

  • 56" x 78"
  • 60" x 78"
  • 66" x 78"
  • 60" x 66" — bathtub
  • 66" x 66" — bathtub

Available Finishes

  • Brass
  • Matte Black
  • Oil Rubbed Bronze

WFS Series

Similar to the DS21 Series, the WFS Series frameless sliding shower door combines a clean, modern aesthetic with smaller hardware. Crafted from 3/8" clear tempered glass, this two-piece system pairs a fixed panel with a sliding door for a sleek, minimalist look.

WS10 Series

The WS10 Series is our value-friendly sliding shower door, designed to deliver a clean, polished look without breaking the budget. Both panels slide for easy access, and the door comes equipped with a framed top rail and a towel bar. Available in 1/4" clear tempered glass for bathtub enclosures or 3/8" clear tempered glass for standing showers, the WS10 Series is a practical and attractive solution for any bathroom.

Available Sizes

  • 60" x 60" — bathtub, 1/4" tempered
  • 66" x 60" — bathtub, 1/4" tempered
  • 48" x 72" — standing shower, 3/8" tempered
  • 56" x 72" — standing shower, 3/8" tempered
  • 60" x 72" — standing shower, 3/8" tempered
  • 66" x 72" — standing shower, 3/8" tempered

Available Finishes

  • Chrome
  • Brushed Nickel
  • Oil Rubbed Bronze
