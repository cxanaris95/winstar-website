About Us

Founded in 1996, Winstar has spent over three decades serving the New York tristate area with expert glass solutions, ranging from windows to shower doors. As a family-owned business based in Flushing, NY, we specialize in manufacturing and producing a wide range of products — including windows, mirrors, shower doors, and metalwork — for both residential and commercial needs.

Quality is at the heart of everything we do. From new window installations to custom shower doors, our team brings industry-level expertise to every project, working closely with you to bring your vision to life. Installation services are available as well.

No job is too big or too small. We also carry an extensive stock of window, shower door, and aluminum door parts. Stop by our showroom or reach out today for a free consultation.
