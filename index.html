01 · Our Story

Three generations
of glasswork.

Founded in 1996, Winstar has spent three decades serving the New York tristate area with expert glass solutions — from windows to shower doors. As a family-owned business based in Flushing, NY, we manufacture and produce a wide range of products, including windows, mirrors, shower doors, and metalwork, for both residential and commercial needs.

Quality is at the heart of everything we do. Our team brings industry-level expertise to every project and works closely with you to bring your vision to life — installation included. No job is too big or too small. Stop by our showroom or reach out today for a free consultation.
